Our Heritage

The Art of
Perfection

A story born in
Southern California

Café Paragon was born from a single obsession: the perfect cup. Inspired by California's thriving coffee culture, we set out to bring that same standard of excellence to Pakistan's most discerning coffee lovers.

We source our beans directly from single-origin farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. Every roast is timed to the second. Every barista completes 200 hours of training before they touch your cup.

From our first location in Lahore to our growing presence across 12 cities, the mission has never changed — we don't just serve coffee, we serve a moment of perfection.
